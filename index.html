Short Stature is a general term for people whose height is considerably below average compared to the height of their peers. While it can apply to adults, the term is more commonly used to refer to children. A child can be significantly shorter than their friends and still be perfectly health. Genetics is a major determinant of height.
Causes/ Effect of these Parameters What can you do about it
  • The major reasons for short stature are: Constitutional Growth Delay- Some children simply develop later than others. These kids are small for their age and often enter puberty later; Genetics- If there are no underlying medical reasons why either parent is short, their child’s short stature may be perfectly healthy. Medical reason like hormonal or endocrinal disorder will present with symptoms like lethargy, fatigue and low concentration.
  • Only a doctor can determine whether your child’s short stature has a medical cause. This process takes time. The doctor will measure your child’s height, weight, limb length and family history. Treatment for short stature depends on the cause (e.g., Thyroid hormone replacement can be used to treat hypothyroidism, Growth hormone injections can treat GHD). For children who are naturally short, no treatment is necessary. Consult your Pediatrician.
